📘 Documentation

Learn how to use SmartAnalytics step-by-step

🚀 Getting Started

  • Go to Dashboard
  • Upload your dataset (CSV / Excel)
  • Wait for processing
  • View analytics instantly

📂 Upload Dataset

Your dataset should contain:

  • Product – Product name
  • Category – Product category
  • Revenue – Sales amount
  • Expense – Cost
  • Tax – Tax applied
  • Date – Transaction date

📊 Dashboard Features

  • 📈 Sales Over Time (Line Chart)
  • 📊 Top Products (Scrollable Bar Chart)
  • 🥧 Category Distribution (Pie Chart)
  • 💰 Tax Distribution (Doughnut Chart)
  • 🧠 AI Insights (Gemini Powered)

🤖 AI Insights

The system uses Google Gemini AI to automatically generate insights from your dataset.

  • Find top-performing products
  • Detect revenue trends
  • Identify business opportunities

🕒 Recent Activity

All uploaded datasets are stored temporarily in your recent activity.

  • Click any dataset → load into dashboard
  • Delete unwanted datasets
  • Data resets on logout

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Upload clean datasets for best results
  • Column names should match required format
  • Large files may take time to process
