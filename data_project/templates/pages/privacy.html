🔒 Privacy Policy

Your data security and privacy are our priority

1. Introduction

SmartAnalytics is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

2. Data Collection

We collect the following data:

  • Uploaded datasets (CSV / Excel)
  • Basic usage activity (dashboard interactions)

3. How We Use Data

  • To generate analytics and charts
  • To provide AI-based insights
  • To improve user experience

4. Data Storage

Uploaded datasets are stored temporarily in the system. Data may be cleared after logout or session reset.

5. Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and users should avoid uploading sensitive information.

6. Third-Party Services

SmartAnalytics uses Google Gemini AI for generating insights. Your data may be processed temporarily by external AI services.

7. User Rights

  • You can delete your datasets anytime
  • You control what data you upload

8. Contact

For any privacy concerns, contact us via the Contact page.
